Instructions

Click 'Start' to read the game instructions.

Click 'Start' again to begin the game.

The level and time limit will be displayed before the game begins.

Press the up arrow to accelerate your car, and long-press it to increase speed. Use the down arrow to slow down.

Navigate through traffic using the left and right arrow keys.

Press the space bar to jump over cars and earn extra points.

Avoid crashes to move to higher levels and earn additional time for each level.
